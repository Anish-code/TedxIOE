About Ted

TED, born in 1984 as a four-day conference uniting innovators in technology, entertainment, and design, has evolved into a global force for change. Its iconic annual conference features concise 18-minute talks, complemented by a vast online library of TEDx Talks, podcasts, and short videos. TED's mission is to discover and spread ideas that inspire imagination, embrace possibilities, and drive meaningful impact.

More Info.

About Tedx

TEDx, an embodiment of TED's "Ideas Worth Spreading," fosters community-driven dialogues independently organized to provide grassroots-level TED-like experiences. These events, aligned with TED's format and guidelines, catalyze meaningful conversations and connections within unique community contexts. TEDx Organizers, motivated by the transformative potential of ideas, operate without political motives or personal profit, requiring a license from TED for one-day events.

More Info.

About Tedx IOE Pulchwok

TEDx IOE Pulchowk, nestled in Kathmandu Valley, Nepal, epitomizes the global TEDx movement as a meticulously planned and community-driven initiative. Operating under a Community License Tier, it embodies open-source principles, fostering local innovation, thought leadership, and visionary insights. Serving as a bridge between the local and global TEDx community, TEDx IOE Pulchowk amplifies transformative ideas from Kathmandu Valley to resonate globally

Tedx Around the Globe

TEDx events, independently organized globally, cover diverse topics with short, powerful talks following the TED format. To find upcoming events, use the search feature on the official TEDx website, considering the dynamic event schedules. These conferences are organized independently, accommodating various themes and speakers for specific community interests. Check the official TEDx website for the latest information on upcoming conferences.

Impactful Talks

TEDx IOE Pulchwok events feature impactful talks that strive to inspire and spark meaningful conversations. Speakers at these events share innovative ideas and experiences to engage and resonate with the audience. The talks often address diverse topics, ranging from technology and science to personal development and social issues. These impactful talks aim to encourage critical thinking and promote positive change within the local community. Attendees can expect a dynamic and thought-provoking experience that leaves a lasting impression and fosters a sense of shared inspiration.

Exposure to International Community

TEDx participation offers international exposure through its globally recognized platform and online sharing of talks. Events showcase speakers with diverse backgrounds, fostering connections with global ideas. Networking opportunities with attendees, speakers, and organizers from different countries enable cross-cultural collaborations and a broader understanding of global challenges and innovations. TEDx serves as a conduit for individuals to engage with the international community and contribute to a global dialogue.
